Project Trash

Fast-paced 3D Arcade Experience

School Project 3 Weeks Unity

Overview

Project Trash is a 3D arcade-style game developed in Unity during a 3-week school project.

What's special :

  • One-hand control with clumsy and quirky physics
  • Sort trash correctly into recycle bins by throwing
  • 6 types of items: glass, metal, paper, plastic, hazardous and general waste
  • Constant flow of trash, increasing over time
  • Timed combo multiplier for consecutive correct sorts and to keep the fast pace
  • 10 mistakes and you‚Äôre fired... Game Over

    The main focus of the project was to create a polished gameplay experience within a short development cycle, emphasizing iteration, clarity, and responsiveness.

    • My Role

    In this project, I was a Game Designer and primarily responsible for:

    • Prototyping: Implemented the core game mechanics and allowed the team to gain an early understanding of the game's overall look and feel.
    • VFX: I was responsible for the VFX of the game as well as various feedback elements such as light indicators.
    • Lighting: I was tasked to make the level feel alive by adding lighting and post process effects.
    • Level Layout: I helped shape the structure and layout of the facility using basic shapes and Unity's ProBuilder tool.
