Witcher III: Revenge Of Ofir

Team Project • 25 Members • RedKit • In Development • Expected 2025–2026

Overview

A large-scale content expansion for The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt, bringing the eastern empire of Ofir to life with new explorable regions, quests, characters, systems and lore. Developed by a team of 25 people using RedKit.

Status: In active development • Estimated full release: Late 2025 / Early 2026

My Role & Contributions

This mod is a collaborative effort with a team of 25 members. My role was strictly Level Designer. I focused exclusively on level design tasks and did not contribute to scripting, quest writing, dialogue, companions, combat mechanics, UI, asset creation, or any other areas.

Level Design Tasks I Handled

  • Blockout and multiple iterations of key explorable zones in the Ofir region
  • Designing gameplay flow, pacing, readability and natural player guidance
  • Implementation of leading lines, landmarks, vistas and environmental storytelling techniques
  • Verticality design, layering and one-way valves to support narrative progression
  • Close collaboration with environment artists and narrative designers to integrate story beats into the spatial experience
  • Regular playtesting sessions and iterative refinement based on team and playtester feedback

All screenshots, videos and level layouts shown in this page represent only the areas and level design work I personally contributed. Quests, dialogue, characters, combat, scripting, new systems and all other content were created by other team members.

Key Features

New Region: Ofir

Unique eastern empire aesthetic with deserts, bazaars, architecture and culture.

Extended Story Content

New main arc + side quests with branching choices and meaningful consequences.

New Gameplay Systems

Expanded crafting, alchemy, unique combat animations and dialogue expansions.

Companions & Factions

Recruitable companions from Ofir and new political factions.
