About Me

First of all, thank you for visiting my portfolio.

For me it all started at the age of 5 when my father brought home an Xbox. From then on, my life has been shaped by experiencing games, and from a very young age, my goal has been to create something truly memorable that people from all over the world can enjoy.

A few things about me : My favourite hobby is hiking and visiting nature. In my free time i enjoy working out, doing outdoor activities and playing games.
